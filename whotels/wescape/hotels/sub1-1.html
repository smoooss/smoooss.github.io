As one of the only Alpine Escapes in North America, W Aspen is poised to carve out a new era of luxury. Our Aspen hotel is nestled at the base of one of the world's most fabled mountains, with access to all its coveted slopes and trails - plus stunning 360° mountain views and striking architectural and interior design. Discover a bold point of view in this iconic town and enjoy a year-round pass to sky-high adventure.

Our slope-side access gets you on the mountain for morning runs and our street-adjacent bike shop can get you outfitted for any style of riding experience. For a more residential experience, The Sky Residences at W Aspen afford an exclusive shared ownership opportunity that includes access to a private, Residences-only 4th floor roof deck and all of W Aspen’s signature amenities. Our boutique hotel is within minutes of many additional outdoor activities, adventure experiences, museums, art galleries and music venues, and high-fashion boutiques. W Aspen is truly an immersive experience.

W Costa Navarino marks the debut of the W Hotels brand in Greece. The new Escape, exclusively for adults and young adults over 12 years old, our 5 star hotel brings contemporary design and inspiring energy to the Navarino Waterfront 13-hectare beachfront site by the Ionian Sea, the newest integrated area of Costa Navarino in Greece.

Vivid and bright, with a distinctive design inspired by the region’s architecture with natural materials like stone, wood, and reed, W Costa Navarino shows stylish accommodations with swim-up rooms, airy one and two-bedroom suites with their own pools, and fantastic beachfront villas with their private pools set against the stunning Ionian sunset. Playful public spaces, five dining venues, including a vibrant Beach Club, a wellness area with AWAY® Spa and FIT gym to fuel body and mind, together with inventive yet authentic cuisine and welcoming service, create a socially driven destination any time of the day.

EXPERIENCES

  • SKIING & SNOWBOARDING

    Come ready to escape in the Rocky Mountains with our over 11,000 foot summit internationally renowned for skiing, snowboarding, hiking, and more. W Aspen is nestled mountainside on Aspen Mountain and perfectly positioned for you to fuel your alpine adventures.

  • SKYHIGH PARAGLIDING

    You can stay in Waspen with Aspen Paraglide, the best paragliding school, and have a pleasant flight experience. Gliding at a breathtaking height while looking at the wonderful scenery can help you get rid of all the stress in your daily life.

  • ROOFTOP SWIMMING

    Enjoy cool swimming as well as great views at the rooftop swimming pool. The glass is all over the place, so you can see the unblocked view, and there is also a parasol with a nice design, so you can relax after swimming.

  • FITNESS CENTER

    With a state-of-the-art FIT fitness center, W Aspen offers many ways to continue your workout while you are on your escape. Work up a sweat on your own with one of many treadmills or bikes, free weights, Peloton bikes, or TRX system.

EXPERIENCES

  • THE WATERSPORTS HUB

    Discover our Watersports hub, the meeting point for on-water and under-water sports fans. From diving and snorkeling or windsurfing and kitesurfing, our Watersports hub is the ideal gateway to indelible recreation "on" or "beneath" of the Ionian Sea.

  • MOURATOGLOU TENNIS CENTER

    At the nearby Navarino Dunes, the first European location for a Mouratoglou Tennis Center offers the opportunity to play on brand new, state-of-the-art courts with breathtaking views of the Ionian Sea, while receiving personalized coaching from the best in the world. The center offers 12 Tennis Courts, 3 Padel Courts & 2 Pickleball Courts.

  • GREECE’S FIRST GOLF COURSE

    Located just a few minutes away from W Costa Navarino, The Dunes Course is the first signature-designed golf course in Greece. This championship course, set in a diverse natural landscape, offers spectacular sea and river views and incorporates some links-style features in areas closest to the sea.

  • FITNESS CENTER

    FUEL is the energy to look good, feel good, go longer and stay later. Open 24/7, this is where state-of-the-art gym equipment, free weights and a medley of private classes with specialized trainers are available for you to FUEL your body.

AN UNFORGETTABLE EXPEDITION

W hotels offers top-notch services and offers a pleasant rest. There are various activities such as swimming pools, spas, and fitness centers that you can enjoy in your accommodation, as well as hot tourist attractions and restaurants located close to the hotel, so it is enough for you to have a new experience.

  • ASPEN

    With a long, storied history, Aspen is an iconic destination for the global jet set. Tucked away in the Rocky Mountains of Colorado, Aspen is a world class alpine playground. Whether you’re visiting to detox, retox, or both, Aspen has a vast variety of experiences to elevate your sky-high escape.
  • ALGARVE

    The southern coast of Portugal is a unique, idyllic region that is bathed by the Atlantic, blessed with gold sanded beaches, surrounded by natural and harmonious landscapes, and enwreathed with picturesque fishing towns. A fusion of modern and traditional, the region provides a variety of scenes and experiences, making the Algarve my hidden paradise!
  • BALI

    Find new life on an island pulsing with energy, ancient culture, and non-stop nightlife. There is nowhere like the island, from its traditional culture, local delicacy, and friendly people; it’s more than a place – it’s a mood, it’s magical, it’s a tropical state of mind.
  • BARCELONA

    Discover a different side of Spain in the capital of Catalunya. Barcelona is an unmistakable escape, with stunning Mediterranean sunrises and electric nights. Enjoy the best tapas, easy going beach life, and never-ending nightlife. Barcelona is here to discover.
  • COSTA RICA

    Soak in the Pura Vida vibe, on the Pacific coast of Costa Rica. Explore exotic beaches & the most sought after surf culture at W Costa Rica – Reserva Conchal. Tucked into the wilderness of a Reserve, you can find how high adrenaline adventures and rich eco-tourism meet in the happiest country on earth.
  • COSTA NAVARINO

    Costa Navarino is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. Remote yet easily accessible, Costa Navarino enjoys a premium location on the Ionian Sea coastline surrounded by pristine land of olive-groves and vineyards.
  • DUBAI

    Dubai is the home of some of the most spectacular skyscrapers, stunning architecture & vibrant hidden gems. Dubai has transformed from a small trading port into a global metropolis in a relatively short span of time.
  • FT LAUDERDALE

    When it comes to Fort Lauderdale, you've got it all, whether it be shopping on Las Olas, soaking up the sun on one of our two WET Decks or dining at dynamic hotspots. There's something for everyone in our city! Escape & Explore our city by chartering a private yacht or by riding around town on one of our beach cruisers.
  • GOA

    Silver sands, sparkling blue waters, the sea mirroring the sky below, white churches resting against green paddy fields and coconut trees lining the coastal streets. Goa - a place you can fall in love with at first glance. There's much more to Goa than sun, sand and sea.
  • IBIZA

    Ibiza is more than an island, it’s a brand. Where flower power meets club culture. Santa Eulalia offers a relaxed pace with a cosmopolitan attitude, with a mix of art galleries, boutiques and amazing dining. And of course, gorgeous beaches abound.
  • KOH SAMUI

    Wherever your exploration takes you on Koh Samui, you’ll never be far from another golden sand beach. The culture gives you a taste of the region’s Asian traditions and unique heritage from hidden Buddhist temples to local fishing villages.
  • MALDIVES

    A surreal island oasis surrounded by lush wildlife and shimmering coral reefs, W Maldives on Fesdu Island is the ultimate off-the-grid escape. Indulge in creative dining, nail your asana on the beach, and get up close and personal with the breathtaking house reefs.
  • MUSCAT

    A place to escape from the everyday. Muscat is a magical city that offers many contrasts between the mountains and ocean. Explore the local culture and be delighted by a combination of traditional and modern lifestyle.
  • PUNTA DE MITA

    Punta de Mita has long been sought out by bohemians and surfers for its pristine waves and natural wonders. The nearby Marietas Islands and local towns like Sayulita and Bucerias capture the best of surf culture and local tradition.
  • SINGAPORE

    Whether you’re visiting for work or play, Singapore has got you covered. From easygoing cafes to bustling nightlife, there's something for everyone. And don't forget about the beaches around Sentosa! Hang out with me to find out more.
  • SOUTH BEACH

    The perfect blend between a dynamic metropolitan city and relaxing Oasis, catering to those who enjoy the best of the day and nightlife. Miami surprises its visitors expecting a laid-back beach town with a glamorous welcome hosting boat shows, fashion shows, food festivals and one of largest art shows in the United States.
  • VERBIER

    Verbier is a vibrant place to escape. In the same day, you can ski, shop, and sip the best cocktails. Visitors and locals alike share a passion for mountain sports and great food. Whatever the season, there's always something new to explore.

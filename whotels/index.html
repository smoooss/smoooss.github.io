WELCOME TO W KOH SAMUI

Hotel guests can start the day at our Koh Samui resort with a workout at FIT or dip at WET before unwinding with a locally inspired therapy at AWAY Spa. Then light up the evening with signature cocktails at WOOBAR and dinner at our hotel's on-site restaurants, Namu and The Kitchen Table. Surrounded by verdant foliage, each of our resort's 74 luxury, pet-friendly villas boasts a private outdoor pool and infinite island views.
WELCOME TO W BALI – SEMINYAK

Our five-star hotel in Seminyak, Bali embodies the archipelago with a rice field-inspired WET pool. Visit our 24-hour AWAY Spa and FIT gym. Experience two of our world-class restaurants with the most popular brunch on the island, Starfish Bloo and FIRE. Join the nightlife at Woobar Bali, a beachfront spot with a magnetic social scene. Enjoy garden and panoramic views of the Indian Ocean in your hotel rooms and suite, or the privacy of your private pool in your villa.

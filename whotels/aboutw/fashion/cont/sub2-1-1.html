SHOP KUALA LUMPUR WITH MODEL ALICIA AMIN

Whether she’s sweating in style for Nike, gracing the screen in the dramatic television show, MY Fullhouse: Alamatnya Cinta, or simply spending the day exploring the diverse fashion scene of Kuala Lumpur, model and actress Alicia Amin dresses for the part. So, of course, that means a few costume changes.

We followed the Asia’s Next Top Model Cycle 5 contestant as she cruised from W Kuala Lumpur (in a pretty fashionable ride at that) to find her looks from delicate to daring at the must-visit shopping spots of Malaysia’s capital.

  • SHOES SHOES SHOES

    Your one-stop shop for the best independent designer fashion and of course, shoes! Stock up on jewelry, dresses, and daring heels to take your style to the next level.

  • ALIA BASTAMAM

    Anything but stuffy, this designer creates formalwear that ticks the boxes for tradition, but can also be a daring statement piece to show off at a party. For any event, whether you have to impress mom or the fashion crowd.

  • ALIA BASTAMAM BRIDAL

    Pull out all the stops on your big day with Alia Basamam’s stunning bridal collection, perfect even if you’re not the one walking down the aisle.
